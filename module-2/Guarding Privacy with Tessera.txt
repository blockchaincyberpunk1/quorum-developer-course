Tessera: The Guardian of Transaction Privacy:

Defining Tessera:

Tessera is a privacy manager integrated with Quorum that handles transaction privacy by encrypting payload data.

The Role of Tessera:

Tessera is responsible for managing the encryption and decryption of private transaction data, ensuring that sensitive information remains confidential.

How Tessera Works:

Tessera utilizes state-of-the-art encryption techniques to secure payload data, preventing unauthorized access and ensuring data privacy.

Encryption and Decryption: A Closer Look at Tessera's Operations:

Transaction Encryption:

Tessera encrypts payload data before it is included in a private transaction, rendering the information unreadable to unauthorized parties.

Transaction Decryption:

Authorized participants use their private keys to decrypt the encrypted payload data, gaining access to the original information.
